The Overlook Hotel

Set majestically in Colorado Provencal in Rustrel, the Overlook Hotel offers a unique experience combining luxury, history, and spectacular ochre landscapes.

Built in 1907, our historic property offers 237 elegantly decorated rooms and suites, each offering a spectacular view of the ochre cliffs and surrounding Provencal countryside.
